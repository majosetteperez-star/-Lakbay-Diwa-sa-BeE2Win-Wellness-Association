A journey of the spirit, toward a healthier community.

Lakbay Diwa sa BeE2Win Wellness Association, Inc. is a community-based, non-government organization building practical wellness pathways — physical, mental, and social — for the people we serve.

Get in touch See our programs

Our mission

"Lakbay Diwa" means a journey of the mind and spirit. We exist to walk that journey with our communities — turning wellness from an abstract goal into daily, attainable practice.

Why we exist

Wellness, made reachable

We bring health education, peer support, and wellness activities directly into barangays and communities that often go without them.

How we work

Community-led

Programs are designed and run with local volunteers and members, not handed down — so they fit the people they serve.

What changes

Lasting habits

We focus on sustainable behavior change: movement, nutrition, mental health awareness, and mutual support that outlasts a single event.

Our programs

The BeE2Win framework — Body, Emotion, Energy — guides everything we run.

Body

Movement & screening

Free community fitness sessions, basic health screenings, and mobility programs for all ages.

Emotion

Mental health support

Peer-support circles and mental wellness orientation sessions, run with partner counselors.

Energy

Nutrition & livelihood

Nutrition education paired with small livelihood and income-support initiatives for member households.

Frequently asked questions

Is Lakbay Diwa sa BeE2Win a registered organization?

Yes. We operate as Lakbay Diwa sa BeE2Win Wellness Association, Incorporated, a community-based non-government organization in the Philippines.

Who can join your programs?

Our programs are open to community members of all ages. Some sessions are designed specifically for seniors, mothers, or youth groups.

Is there a cost to participate?

Most community sessions are free or low-cost. Specific program fees, if any, are explained when you sign up.

How can I support or partner with the organization?

We welcome volunteers, in-kind donations, and partnerships with organizations aligned with community wellness. Reach out using the contact details below.
